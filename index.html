About Me

 My name is Ian Cogswell. I grew up in Massachusetts and moved to New Hampshire shortly after High-School. After High-School I got into gaming (who didn't) and got to go "pro" for a Summer. I eventually found friends who shared similar passions in art and websites. I had previously built websites in MSPaint when I was a wee lad so I choose to follow my passions. My passion has always been in art and design and I have continued that passion into coding. For awhile I created my own company, Aroarian Studios, and was successful for a year. I got a few customers but my passions shifted and my focus went to a more stable career. I found myself working for Liberty Mutual and became a Business Systems Analyst which lead me back to my passions.

 My coding experience has grown over the years picking up tid bits of information here or there or learning the basics of a new language. I have had the pleasure to work in Java, Javascript, HTML, CSS, SQL, VisualBasic and Selenium and I can't wait to learn more!
